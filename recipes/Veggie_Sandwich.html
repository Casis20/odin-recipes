The Vegetarian Sandwich

This sandwich provides the perfect healthy meal for people who for any reason don't eat meat!

There is something special about a sandwich that is just packed with vegetables.

Ingredients

For the Pickles:

  • 1 cup of sliced cucumber
  • 1 medium julienned carrot
  • 1 cup of rice vinegar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 table spoons of kosher salt

For the Miso Eggplant:

  • 1 medium eggplant, sliced into 1.2 inch slices
  • 1 tablespoon of kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon of honey

For the Sandwiches:

  • 2 avocados
  • 1/2 cup of lime juice
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 thick sliced heirloom tomatoes
  • 2 cups of arugula
  • 2 thinly sliced small shallots
  • 2 cups of alfafa sprouts
  • 1/2 cup of hummus
  • 8 slices of whole wheat bread

Steps

For the Pickles:

  1. In a medium bowl combine the cucumber and carrots.
  2. Cover with rice vinegar, water and salt.
  3. Stir until salt is fully dissolved.
  4. Cover with plastic wrap and let mariante in the fridge for at least 20 min and up to 48 hours.

for the Miso Eggplant:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Lay the eggplant slices out on a cutting board and sprinkle all sides with kosher salt.
  3. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  4. In a small bowl whisk miso, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil and honey.
  5. Line a sheet tray with parchment paper.
  6. Dip each slice of eggplant, on both sides, in the miso glaze and arrange them on the sheet tray without overlaping them.
  7. Place the tray in the oven and roast for 30 minutes, flipping halfway through until browned and tender.

For the Sandwiches:

  1. In a small bowl scoop out the flesh of the avocados and add lime juice.
  2. Mash into a spread, seasoning with salt and pepper to your taste.
  3. Sprinkle the tomato slices with a touch of salt.
  4. Start building the sandwich by spreading 1/4 of the avocado on one slice of bread and 2 tablespoons of hummus on the other.
  5. Begin with the avocado slice then stack the vegetables in the following order:
    • Couple of slices of the miso eggplant.
    • handful of arugula
    • 2 slices of heirloom tomato
    • Pickled cucumbers
    • Carrots
    • Shallots
    • Sprouts
