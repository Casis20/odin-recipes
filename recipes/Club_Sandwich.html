The Club Sandwich

With ham, bacon, and turkey divided by 3 slices of bread, the club sandwich could possibly be the best sandwich of all time.

Ingredients

  • 3 pieces of sliced bread
  • 3 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 tomato slices
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 pieces of cooked bacon
  • 1 slice of cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of turkey
  • 2 slices of your choice of ham

Steps

  1. Toast bread until golden, then spread a thin layer of butter on both sides.
  2. Spread the mayonnaise on one side of one slice of bread.
  3. Top with the lettuce and tomato slices, then season lighlt with salt and pepper.
  4. Place bacon slices on top.
  5. Spread mayonnaise on both sides of a second piece of bread and place on top of bacon.
  6. Top with cheddar cheese, turkey, and ham.
  7. Spread mayonnaise on the last piece of sliced bread and place on top of sandwich.
  8. Secure with toothpicks and cut into triangles.
