Ham & Cheese Sandwich Recipe

The Ham & Cheese Sandwich an all time classic

Originating in the UK, the Ham & Cheese Sandwich became a staple food for many people to eat on the go around the world!

Its basic ingredients and quick preparation makes this recipe a go to choice if you want a quick and delicious meal.

Ingredients Needed

  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of mustard
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 6 slices of Swiss cheese
  • 6 slices of your choice of ham
  • 8 slices of dill pickle

Steps to follow

  1. Spread one side of each slice of bread with mayonnaise. In a small bowl, stir to combine the mustard and honey.
  2. On 2 of the 4 slices of bread, spread the mustard mixture on the opposite side of the mayo.
  3. Top with 3 slices of cheese and 3 slices of ham per sandwich.
  4. Add 4 slices of pickle each, then top with remaining slice of bread, with mayo side out.
  5. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  6. Add the sandwiches and cook until bread is golden and cheese is melted, at least 3 minutes per side.
